  • DG Visit to CSIR-IIIM, UT of J & K

    DG CSIR visited the Janaki Ammal herbarium in CSIR-IIIM, Jammu. She spent part of her career there in 1960's. She is a well known Indian botanist who conducted scientific research in cytogenetics and phytogeography.

  • Dr. Shekhar C Mande, Secretary, today called on Lieutenant Governor Manoj Sinha at Raj Bhavan.

    Dr. Mande who is also the Director General, Council of Scientific and Industrial Research discussed with the Lt Governor a variety of Scientific and technological solutions those can be brought to UT of J&K so that people can be benefitted from the technology-led solutions. Discussions were also took place on several wide-ranging issues pertaining to conservation of environment and water bodies, employment generation, and productive utilization of human resources of the region.
